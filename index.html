About Us

Binaplus Enterprises and N'Royal Enterprises are leading manufacturing companies based in Ese Odo Local Government, Ondo State. With our strategic location at No 36 Broad Street, Sabomi, we have established ourselves as reliable partners for businesses seeking quality manufactured products. Our state-of-the-art facilities combine modern manufacturing techniques with experienced craftsmanship to deliver products that meet international standards. We are committed to supporting Nigeria's industrial growth by providing locally manufactured solutions that compete globally while creating employment opportunities in our community.
